Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Kitchen Weight Scale Insight app (the "Application") for Android mobile devices, developed by Kitchen digital scale, scales weight assistant (the "Service Provider") as an ad-supported service. This service is provided "AS IS".

This policy is effective as of 2026-05-08.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to collect as little data as possible. It does not require user registration, account creation, or any form of login. Below is a description of data that the Application and its integrated third-party SDKs and services may automatically process.

1.1 Data Automatically Processed

When you use the Application, the following categories of data may be automatically collected or processed by the Application, its backend, or integrated third-party SDKs:

  • Device or other identifiers: an app-local installation identifier (a randomly generated ID created at first install, not linked to your name or account), advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID / GAID, where available and subject to your device settings and consent), and app set identifier or ad services identifiers where permitted by the platform.
  • Approximate location: inferred from your IP address or SDK signals at the country/region level. The Application does not request or use GPS or any precise location permission.
  • Device and app information: device model, manufacturer, operating system version, app version, and app identifier.
  • App interactions and activity: events such as lesson starts and completions, quiz interactions, onboarding selections, screen views, category navigation, achievement events, notification/reminder interactions, and similar in-app actions. These events are sent to the Application's backend and to AppMetrica analytics.
  • Session data: session duration and frequency of app use.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics: crash reports, ANR data, performance metrics, and other diagnostic information collected by AppMetrica to maintain app stability.
  • Ad-related data: ad impressions, ad interactions (views, clicks), ad network and placement identifiers, CPM/revenue signals, and fraud-prevention signals, processed by CAS.AI mediation, Google Mobile Ads/AdMob, and their partner ad networks.
  • Backend request metadata: locale setting, units preference, and app version sent when the Application fetches remote configuration and content. Cloudflare, as the hosting infrastructure provider, may also process IP address and network routing data for security and service operation purposes.

2. How Information Is Used

Data collected or processed is used for the following purposes:

  • App functionality: delivering learning content, quizzes, achievements, and remote configuration to your device.
  • Analytics: understanding how users interact with the Application in order to improve it, monitor stability, and analyze audience behavior.
  • Advertising: displaying relevant ads to support the free operation of the Application. Ad partners may use identifiers and interaction data to serve ads and measure their effectiveness.
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance: detecting invalid traffic, protecting ad integrity, and ensuring the Application operates securely.

Data transmitted to the Application's backend (https://kitchen-scale-api.kitchenscaleapi.workers.dev/) is linked to a pseudonymous installation identifier, not to any personally identifiable information such as your name or email. All communication with the backend is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

4. Data Sharing

The Service Provider does not sell your personal data to third parties. Data may be shared in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party SDKs and services listed in Section 3, as described in this policy.
  • As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process.
  • When necessary to protect the rights, safety, or security of users or third parties, or to respond to a government request.
  • With service providers who work on the Service Provider's behalf and are contractually bound to handle data in accordance with this policy.

5. Opt-Out and User Controls

  • Advertising ID: You can reset or opt out of interest-based advertising by adjusting your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads on Android 12+, or Google Settings → Ads on earlier versions).
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications at any time in your device's notification settings for the app.
  • Uninstall: You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application. All locally stored data (progress, settings) will be removed from your device. Pseudonymous data already transmitted to analytics or backend services may be retained for a limited period as described in Section 6.

6. Data Retention

Locally stored data (lesson progress, settings) is retained on your device until you clear the app's data or uninstall it.

Data transmitted to AppMetrica and the Application's backend server is retained in pseudonymous form for as long as necessary for analytics and app improvement, subject to AppMetrica's and Cloudflare's own data retention policies.

The Application does not currently offer an in-app mechanism to request deletion of backend data. If you have questions about data held by the Service Provider, please contact: tina.dev.scales@gmail.com

7. Children

The Application is not directed at children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact the Service Provider at tina.dev.scales@gmail.com, and the necessary steps will be taken promptly.

8. Security

All data transmitted between the Application and the backend server is encrypted in transit using HTTPS (TLS). The Service Provider takes reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed through the Application and its third-party services. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.
